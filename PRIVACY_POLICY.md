# Privacy Policy for Awaria

## Last Updated: April 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **Awaria** ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information.

## 1. Information We Collect

**Awaria** is a privacy-first application.

- **Personal Data**: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data (such as your name, email, or phone number) to our servers.
- **Location Data**: You provide location information (City and Street) within the App to receive relevant alerts. This information is **stored locally on your device only**. It is never uploaded to any cloud service or shared with third parties.
- **Usage Data**: We do not track your usage of the App.

## 2. Third-Party Services

The App fetches data from various utility providers (Tauron, Energa, Enea, PGE, Stoen, Fortum, MPWiK). When fetching this data, your device makes direct requests to their public APIs. These providers may see your IP address as part of the standard HTTP request, but no other identifying information is sent.

## 3. Data Storage

All settings, monitored addresses, and alert history are stored exclusively in the App's local storage on your device. Clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App will permanently delete this information.

## 4. Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at: <contact@eremef.xyz>.
