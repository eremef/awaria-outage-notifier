A modern application providing real-time alerts for planned maintenance and emergency utility outages. Awaria aggregates data from multiple utility providers into a single, intuitive interface.

Supported Sources:
- Power: Tauron, Energa, Enea, PGE, Stoen
- Heat: Fortum
- Water: MPWiK (currently Wrocław area)

Key Features:
- Multi-Address Support: Monitor up to 20 different locations simultaneously.
- Smart Address Matching: Automatically highlights alerts affecting your specific street.
- Real-time Notifications: Receive instant alerts on your mobile device.
- Android Widgets: Compact 1x1 widgets for one-tap status checks.
- Privacy First: No cloud accounts required. Your location and settings stay on your device.

Download Awaria today and stay informed about utility issues in your area!
